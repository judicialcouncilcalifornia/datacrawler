Location & Contact Info
Print


The Mariposa Superior Court building is a FULL SECURITY facility. All persons entering the buildings will be required to pass through a weapons screening device and have all hand-carried objects x-rayed. Shorts, tank tops, crop tops and bare feet are not permitted in any of the Mariposa Superior Court facilities. Read more information about Court Etiquette and Security.

Parking: Parking can be found on all sides of the Courthouse and is free.
Access:

The Courthouse can be accessed via the main entrance facing Bullion Street.

ADA access can be found at the rear entrance facing Jones Street.
