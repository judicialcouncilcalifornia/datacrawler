    Online Services
     

    Access Court Services Online

    The Superior Court of California, County of Stanislaus provides many online resources to meet your needs. Please make your selection from the list of services below.

     

    Tentative Rulings - CIVIL
    Click the button to access tentative rulings issued by Civil Departments 21, 22, 23 & 24 which are accessible after 1:30 p.m. on the court day before the hearing.    		 Access Now
    Online Case Index
    On this site you will be able to search certain Court records, locate case names and view some filed documents.    		 Access Now
    Jury Duty
    Select this service if you have received a jury summons and want to respond or update your information or want to check your status or learn who is on call this week.    		 Access Now
    Pay Traffic Tickets (Moving Violations)
    If you received a citation for a moving violation you can pay your ticket online. You can also register to attend traffic school.    		 Access Now

     

    Tentative Rulings - FAMILY
    Click the button to access tentative rulings issued by Family Law Departments 9, 13, 14 & 25 which are accessible after 1:30 p.m. on the court day before the hearing.    		 Access Now
    Court Calendar
    A daily directory for the next 5-6 court days is available.  Hearings are grouped by day and are in alphabetical order.    		 Access Now
    Mediator/ADR Panel
    Click the button to view a list of Court approved Civil Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) providers or judicial arbitrators.    		 Access Now
    Court Reporter Transcript
    Request a copy of a court reporter transcript.    		 Access Now
